Leo is an upcoming Indian Tamil-language film directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, who co-wrote the script with Rathna Kumar and Deeraj Vaidy. It is produced by S. S. Lalit Kumar, under Seven Screen Studio, and co-produced by Jagadish Palanisamy. The film stars Vijay and Trisha while Sanjay Dutt (in his Tamil debut), Arjun, Gautham Vasudev Menon, Priya Anand, Mysskin and Mansoor Ali Khan play supporting roles.

The film is rumored to be a part of the Lokesh Cinematic Universe. It is scheduled to release on 19th October 2023 coinciding with Dusshera along with a simultaneous release in Telugu, Hindi and Kannada dubbed versions.
